Azure Nigeria User Group, Lagos, Nigeria

All around the world user groups and communities want to learn about Azure and Cloud Computing!
This group is for anyone interested in Azure Cloud technologies and offerings as well as Microsoft.

This is a group for anyone interested in Azure Cloud, every month we organize Azure meetup. At least 70% of the talks are about Azure technology or automating, securing, optimizing Azure services. We are a community that believes and act in Microsoft's mission statement “to empower every person and every organization on the planet to achieve more.”

We are organising an event and looking for people all over the Globe to take part.


We also look for contributions on any Azure Topic, don't be put off if someone is already doing that topic.
Your take on the subject will be different, and we encourage all contributions.

Submit your topic here.


Note: In case you want to contribute with a Live session, please contact David Dennis .

What we provide

  • A dedicated Youtube Channel to host your video contribution(s).
  • Support to create pre-recorded or Live stream content.
  • A community ready to hear from you.

Our Ask

  • Join the Go Azure Nigeria User Group - Slack Channel if you haven't already.
  • Join the Go Azure Nigeria User Group - whatsapp Group if you haven't already.
  • A video contribution, let's say 15-45 min video on an Azure topic.
  • We will host this and supply a OneDrive link for you to upload too, if needed.

Why Join Us

  • An opportunity to take part in on-premise or a virtual Azure community event
  • Learn from the best in the industry
  • Discover the best tools and practices within Azure
  • Share content with developers from Nigeria and all over the world
  • Grow your network
